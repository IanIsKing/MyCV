About Me

My Career Success

As a Seasoned Entrepreneur and Mechatronics Engineer, I've developed a diverse portfolio of successful businesses, each recognized for its innovation, excellence, and industry leadership. At the forefront is my manufacturing business, the heart of our operation, serving all nine provinces in South Africa. We've achieved market dominance through our pioneering use of proprietary technology, a high degree of automation, and an efficient logistics network supported by our own fleet of vehicles.

Complementing this, Im a partner in a cutting-edge software business dedicated to Software as a Service (SAAS) solutions for business automation. This platform drives efficiency and productivity across various sectors. Rounding out my portfolio, I oversee a lucrative property business and an asset firm, each contributing to a robust, diversified business ecosystem.
